Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do your activities target?

Our activities are designed for children aged 3-12 years. Each activity specifies the appropriate age range, and many can be adapted for different skill levels.

Do you offer in-person classes?

Yes, we offer hands-on math classes in select locations in Bangalore based on demand and community interest. If you're interested in bringing our classes to your area, please contact us.

How do I access the free downloadable activities?

Free downloadable activities are available on our Activities page. Simply click on an activity marked "Free Download," enter your email, and you'll receive immediate access to the download.

Do you provide resources for schools or teachers?

Yes, many of our activities are suitable for classroom use. We also offer workshops for teachers and can create custom resources for schools. Please contact us for more information about educational partnerships.

How often do you add new activities?

We add new activities on a regular basis, typically 2-3 new activities each month. Subscribe to our newsletter to be notified when new resources are available.
